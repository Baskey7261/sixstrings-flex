Grading System:

In accordance with the Trinity College of London's Classical & Rock-shool, grade initial through to grade 8 syllabuses.



The grading system begins at grade initial for beginners and continues through to grade 8. Every pupil at Musical Six Strings will undergo an initial assessment to determine their current grade level in accordance with, the Trinity College of London (TCL) syllabus.

Grades 6-8 are a minimum requirement for students hoping to be put forward for further education such as a diploma or higher. We encourage our pupils to work toward completing each grade, however, we do also cater to pupils that are just aiming to hone their current skills and expand on their musical repertoire.

Our Syllabuses:

We run two main syllabuses, Classical & Rock School; although, we do offer classes in flamenco, ukulele, or none instrument-specific lessons in music theory and sight-reading.

Our curriculum aims to not only expand on the student's guitar playing technique, but to also provide in-depth knowledge of the instrument they are playing along with musical notation and the ability to improvise fluently and creatively in response to unexpected musical stimuli.

Classical:

Designed around the Trinity College of London's Classical & Plectrum syllabus. From initial to grade eight.



The Classical syllabus covers a wide range of repertoire from all genres of classical music. The technical work includes uniquely composed exercises to ensure the required technique is in place to complete each grade.

Students will be trained through the early grades with exercises designed to build on the skill level and technical ability which will be relied upon heavily, during the later grades.

Read more...

Rock School:

Designed around the Trinity College of London's Rock and Pop syllabus (2018-2024).



The Rock School syllabus focuses on merging the techniques required for both acoustic and electric guitar. The aim of the syllabus is to build a skill set that will be useful in building a repertoire that suits both instruments,

with an emphasis on a variety of finger and plectrum techniques. Students will be encouraged to explore a wide range of styles including, rock-and-pop, jazz, blues, and country. The syllabus also covers chord progressions and patterns, along with standard and none-standard, scales and arpeggios, incorporating the use of both plectrum and fingers.
